          <h1>Research Interests</h1>
          <p>
            The primary objective of my research is to develop new statistical theories and methodologies for large-scale and high-dimensional data with complex structures. My research has focused on high dimensional/large-scale statistical inference, kernel and distance-based methods, genomics and microbiome data analysis, functional data analysis, time series, and econometrics. My research is supported by NIH, NSF, and local grants from Texas A&M.
          </p>
